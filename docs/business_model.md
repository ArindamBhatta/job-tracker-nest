# 💼 Business Model & Vision

## 🎯 The Vision
A centralized platform that simplifies the job search process for candidates and the hiring process for employers.

## 👤 Target Users
1.  **Job Seekers**: Students or professionals looking for career opportunities.
2.  **Employers**: HR managers and Recruiters looking for the best talent.
3.  **Interviewers**: External or internal experts evaluating candidates.

## 💎 Core Values
- **Transparency**: Clear tracking of application status.
- **Rich Profiles**: Beyond just a resume—tracking education, certifications, and skills in detail.
- **Efficient Hiring**: Streamlining the interview and feedback loop.
